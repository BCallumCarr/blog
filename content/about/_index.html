Hi there!

My name is Brad Carruthers and I’m currently completing an MSc Statistics (Research) degree at the London School of Economics and Political Science. You’re welcome to have a look at my cv, my LinkedIn profile and you might also want to have a look at what I’ve been doing on GitHub.

I started this blog in January 2019, so it is most likely still a bit barren. My plan is to populate it with things that I’m currently doing and that I’m interested in. Speaking of things I’m interested in: I enjoy surfing, teaching, public speaking, travelling and I’m passionate about helping solve challenges in developing countries and globally - inequality, chronic poverty, online privacy and the implications of the fourth industrial revolution are currently at the top of my list.

I have experience as an investment analyst, an Economics lecturer (Stellenbosch University in South Africa) and a Mathematics teaching assistant (also Stellenbosch University). Recently, I’ve worked as both an Academic Manager and Data Science teaching assistant at iXperience, an innovative EdTech company based in Cape Town, South Africa.
