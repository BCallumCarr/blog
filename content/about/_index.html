Hi there!

My name is Brad Carruthers and I’m currently completing an MSc Statistics (Research) degree at the London School of Economics and Political Science. You’re welcome to have a look at my CV, my LinkedIn profile and you might also want to have a look at what I’ve been doing on GitHub.

Having started this blog in January 2019, it is still in its infancy stages - my plan is to populate it with things that I’m currently doing and that I’m interested in. Speaking of things I’m interested in: out of the office you might find me surfing, teaching, public speaking, playing Dungeons and Dragons or travelling. I believe capitalism, the free market and competition is the key to addressing challenges in developing countries and around the world. I’m passionate about education, digital privacy and the implications of the fourth industrial revolution.

I have experience as an investment analyst, an Economics lecturer (Stellenbosch University in South Africa) and recently I was an Academic Manager and taught Data Science at iXperience, an innovative EdTech company based in Cape Town.

Feel free to pop a mail to my school address: b{dot}c{dot}carruthers{at}lse{dot}ac{dot}uk to get a hold of me. Last but definitely not least, I thank my wife Bronwyn for her input on all that I write here, and for her consistent support and patience while I follow my passion.
